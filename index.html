This website is meant to serve as a digital portfolio showcasing some of the things I have made on Photoshop over the last year. From having never used Photoshop before to the creation the numerous compositions I can now put my name to, this portfolio chronicles some of the best pieces I have made during my journey of learning to utilize the elements and principles of design in Photoshop.
