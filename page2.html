Squids

In this piece, I was inspired by the incredible sense of movement that is often exuded by sea creatures. As a result, I took the squids and their interesting shapes, and added a dark, mysterious environment to contrast the vibrance of their forms painted with colorful flair.

Birch Trees

This project was originally meant to serve as a simple, minimalist background of birch trees in the summer. However, I saw its potential to become a window into a gorgeous summer day, and brought that potential to fruition through this composition.

Rhythms

The goal of this project was to create various impressions of rhythm, and this project achieves that goal beautifully. Combining various textures and colors, I created several squares of raw visual impressions.
